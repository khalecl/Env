Strategic Case for Scope 1&2 Only

  • Cannot control customer combustion decisions
  • Supply cuts shift production to higher-carbon producers
  • Operational excellence is within company control
  • Focus where impact is measurable and attributable
  • Reduces gaming and double-counting in markets

Case for Full Accountability

  • 96% of emissions excluded from climate targets
  • Business model fundamentally depends on combustion
  • Paris Agreement requires value chain action
  • Major competitors (Shell, BP, TotalEnergies) report Scope 3
  • Market pressure from institutional investors increasing
